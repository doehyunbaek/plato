How to use
  • Choose a translation language, then pick a Stephanus page.
  • Hover a phrase to highlight the approximate matching phrase on the other side.
  • Click a phrase to lock the highlight; click again to clear it.
  • Use search to find passages across Greek and all loaded translations.
